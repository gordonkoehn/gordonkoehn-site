About Us

Push your boundaries

NyxX Studios is a team of developers which was founded in February 2015. We are five students from Hamburg (Germany) who are all interested in modelling and programming. We started our first project called 'Linearity' in February and wanted to publish it for Android, iOS and Windows Phone by the end of summer vacation. By now we have done it for Android.

We already have some ideas for PC games in mind! Even through we are still beginners, we try to catch up tech.

Thanks to the digital world, it makes learning easier in every way!
